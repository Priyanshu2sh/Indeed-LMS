Cancellation and Refund Policy

Effective Date: 1 August 2025

We understand that plans may change. Below are our policies regarding cancellations and refunds:

1. Training Programs / Workshops
  • Cancellation requests made at least 7 days before the start of the program are eligible for a full refund.
  • No refunds will be provided for cancellations made less than 7 days before program start.
2. Online Courses / Digital Products

Once accessed or downloaded, these are non-refundable unless there is a proven technical issue.

3. Internship Enrollments

Any paid fees for internship processing are non-refundable once the process has begun.

4. How to Request a Refund

Email us at info@indeedinspiring.com with your name, course details, and payment reference.
